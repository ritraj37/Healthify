When to Call Emergency Services

Heart Attack

Chest pain, shortness of breath, nausea, sweating

Stroke

Sudden weakness, speech problems, facial drooping

Breathing Problems

Severe difficulty breathing, choking, allergic reactions

Severe Injuries

Major trauma, severe bleeding, broken bones
