Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, such as when you create an account, book appointments, or contact us. This includes personal information like name, email, phone number, and medical information necessary for treatment.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide medical services and patient care
  • Schedule and manage appointments
  • Communicate about your health and treatments
  • Improve our services and website functionality
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We may share information with healthcare providers involved in your care, insurance companies for billing purposes, and as required by law.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All medical records are encrypted and stored securely.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You may also request a copy of your medical records or restrict certain uses of your information.

Contact Us
